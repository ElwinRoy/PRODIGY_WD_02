Welcome to Stopwatch

Uses:

  • Timing activities during workouts or exercises
  • Measuring time for cooking or baking
  • Tracking time for presentations or speeches
  • Monitoring time intervals during games or sports

Functions:

  • Start/Stop: Begin or pause the stopwatch
  • Lap: Record a lap time while the stopwatch is running
  • Reset: Clear all lap times and reset the stopwatch
